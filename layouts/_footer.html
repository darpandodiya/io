Linenoise (Danne Stayskal) is synaesthetic—she sees what she hears and plays what looks good.

She brings decades of classical training to her love of electronic music, performing around Cascadia and regularly at Burning Man. Her name comes from the BBS and phone phreaker communities in Texas in 1994 where she was known for her noisy phone lines. The name stuck with her. As a founding member of the Tautology collective, she builds on her passions for math and science to keep new music flowing through the Seattle and Portland tech sectors, music festivals, and warehouse parties.

She has played alongside and opened for Filastine, bvdub, Boreal Taiga, Luke Mandala, Archivist, Ovoid, Erothyme, Bodicypha, Radiance, Leave Trace, Eugene Fauntleroy, Orqid, Michael Manahan, Nancy Dru, Dave Pezzner, Night Train, Manos, DJ Care, Desert Dwellers, Kaminanda, Yaima, Hanssen, and many others. She is also a regular on a few radio shows, including Spoondrift and SPiN SPiN. Her sets have charted globally on Mixcloud in electronica, techno, downtempo, and ambient.

Through many genres, she brings the style of someone who sees music differently.

This site is open source; its source code is available on github.

Based in Seattle, Cascadia and Black Rock City, Nevada
