Linenoise (Danne Stayskal) is synaesthetic—she sees what she hears and plays what looks good.

She brings decades of classical training to her love of electronic music, performing around Cascadia and regularly at Burning Man. And as a founding member of the Tautology mathematician-artist collective, she couples her passions for music and technology to keep new music flowing through the Seattle and Portland tech sectors, music festivals, and warehouse parties.

She has played alongside and opened for Filastine, bvdub, Boreal Taiga, Luke Mandala, Archivist, Ovoid, Erothyme, Leave Trace, Eugene Fauntleroy, Orqid, Michael Manahan, Nancy Dru, Dave Pezzner, Manos, Desert Dwellers, Kaminanda, Yaima, Hanssen, and many others. She is also a regular on a few radio shows and pirate radio stations, including Spoondrift, SPiN SPiN, and Shady FM. Her sets have charted globally on Mixcloud in electronica, techno, experimental, downtempo, and ambient.

Through many genres, she brings the style of someone who sees music differently.

This site is open source; its source code is available on github.

Based in Seattle, Cascadia and Black Rock City, Nevada
